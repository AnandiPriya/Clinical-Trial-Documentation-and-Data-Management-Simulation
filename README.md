## Project Outcome

This repository simulates a complete clinical research workflow, including protocol development, informed consent, case report form design, clinical data management, adverse event monitoring, regulatory compliance review, and final study reporting.

The project demonstrates practical skills relevant to Clinical Research Associate (CRA), Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC), Clinical Trial Assistant (CTA), Clinical Data Management Associate, and Clinical Research Analyst positions.
